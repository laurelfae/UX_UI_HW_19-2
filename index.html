Read more about me here!

I’m Laurel Lupien, a user experience designer & visual storyteller who believes in the powerful impact design can have on people’s lives. 

With a background in photography, I put an emphasis on the visual language of projects I work on, informing the rest of my design process. My approach is playful, engaging, and detail-oriented. I centre my users’ needs and emotions associated with their experience so I can make it the best experience possible!


My UX Skills

My Philosophy

I love asking questions like: “How can I tell this story?” “What should it feel like?” “How does this story solve the user’s problem?” Creating a lasting impact out of a user experience, no matter how big or small, is what I love.

Technical Skills

Technologies and skills I regularly employ are Figma, user research & interviews, colour theory, typography, UI grids, design thinking, iconography, and more! With a love of learning, I’m always expanding my skillset.

Design Process

I put the user’s experience above anything else in my process, and this includes user research. I use user interviews and information gathered from them to create insights to work with.
